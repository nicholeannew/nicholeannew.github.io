About Me

Nichole grew up dancing from the age of three in Arizona. She was part of an elite competition team through high school. She then went on to be a part of the dance team at Arizona State University. At the age of 19 she knew that she wanted to make her passion her career and moved to Los Angeles. Nichole has been working professionally in the dance industry for the past ten years. She has danced for artists such as Bette Midler, Beyonce, Pink, Jennifer Hudson, The weeknd, Jennifer Lopez, Depeche Mode, Robin Thicke, and Kanye West. She has appeared on Ellen, Mad Tv, Americas Got Talent, and Dick Clarks Rockin New Years Eve. She has worked for brands such as Mac Cosmetics, Skechers, Toms shoes, and Coca Cola. She has also performed around the world with latin artist Chayanne. Nichole is still working professionally and loves that she gets to do what she loves as her career while also inspiring the younger generation.
